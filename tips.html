Important reminders:

  1. Stand behind the yellow line especially when a train is arriving.
  2. Always report suspicious behavior.
  3. Follow all the warning signs and signals.
  4. Stay alert and pay attention to all announcements.

Helpful tips for your next train ride:

  1. Arrive early and avoid rush hour. Rush hour is usually when many students and employees travel so expect it to be very crowded.
  2. Familiarize yourself with the route you’ll be taking and know what your stop is. You wouldn’t want to miss your stop and lose a significant amount of time.
  3. Keep or hide your belongings. There are possible pickpockets so putting your valuables that could be easy to steal inside your bag or close to your chest is ideal.
  4. Prevent from standing near the door as there'll be lots of people going in and out of the train.
  5. Keep calm and ride the train. Riding the MRT can be scary especially for first-timers but if you’ll stay calm and have a good knowledge of how everything works, you’ll be fine.
